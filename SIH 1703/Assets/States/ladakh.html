Ladakh

Ladakh has been a Union Territory since 2019, it's still relatively new, and its laws and amendments are evolving continuously.

Key laws and amendments for Ladakh:

1. Central Laws and Amendments

⁕ Union Territory Act, 2019: This act established Ladakh as a Union Territory, granting it a separate administrative structure.
⁕ Jammu and Kashmir Reorganization Act, 2019: This act revoked Article 370, which granted special status to Jammu and Kashmir, including Ladakh. It also divided the state into two Union Territories.

2. Specific Laws and Amendments for Ladakh

⁕ Ladakh Autonomous Hill Development Council: This council has been established to promote the development of Ladakh. Its powers and functions are outlined in specific laws and amendments.
⁕ Ladakh Tenancy Act: This act may regulate land tenancy and agricultural practices in Ladakh.
⁕ Ladakh Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act: Ladakh has its own GST laws, which may differ slightly from the national GST.
